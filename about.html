Our Story

Made by college students for pet lovers everywhere

It Started in a Dorm Room

We're a group of college students who missed our pets while away at school. Every time we looked at photos of our dogs and cats, we wished we could keep them close somehow.

That's when furever was born. We wanted to create something more than just another t-shirt brand. We wanted to create a way for people to carry the love of their pets with them, wherever life takes them.

At furever, we don't just make clothes. We create something personal - a way to keep your furry best friend close to your heart, literally.

Pet Lovers First

Every design is made with love and understanding of the bond between pets and their humans.

Student Made

Run by students who understand Gen Z style, comfort, and what makes clothing special.

Quality Crafted

Premium materials, careful printing, and attention to detail in every piece we create.
