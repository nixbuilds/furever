Our Story

Made by college students for pet lovers everywhere

FurEver

Welcome to FurEver — a brand built on the belief that fashion can do more than just look good; it can create real impact. FurEver was started by two friends, Nikhil and Shaunak with a shared love for animals and a simple idea — to turn creativity into something meaningful We’re not here to simply sell merchandise. We’re here to build a movement where every purchase contributes to supporting animals in need across India. At FurEver, each product tells a story of compassion. A portion of every sale goes directly toward helping rescue, care for, and protect animals, turning everyday fashion into a force for good. When you wear FurEver, you’re not just expressing your style — you’re standing for something bigger. We also proudly collaborate with NGOs and animal welfare organizations, acting as a strategic partner to help amplify their reach and fundraising efforts. We understand the challenges these organizations face, and our goal is to provide seamless support so they can focus on what truly matters — saving and improving animal lives. As we continue to grow, we are actively seeking partnerships with NGOs who share our vision of creating a kinder world for animals. Together, we can redefine purpose-driven commerce and make compassion a part of everyday life. Join us on this journey — because every choice you make can make a difference.
